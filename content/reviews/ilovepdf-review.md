## Quick take

iLovePDF's standout strength is breadth — merge, split, compress, rotate, watermark, page numbers, PDF-to-everything, and more, nearly all available without creating an account. For a free tool, processing quality on our test files was consistently solid.

## What we liked

- **No-login access** to the vast majority of tools, even for repeated use.
- **Wide tool selection** — including useful niche utilities like watermarking and page numbering that many competitors charge for.
- **Reliable processing**, even on our larger test files, with no noticeable drop in output quality.
- **Free tier is generous** compared to most of the category.

## Where it could improve

- **Interface density** — with so many tools on offer, the homepage can feel busier than more streamlined competitors, especially on mobile.
- **Visual consistency** varies slightly between tools, a side effect of the toolset growing organically over the years.

## How it compares

If your priority is having every PDF tool available for free without an account, iLovePDF is one of the strongest options we tested. If you mainly do the "core five" and prefer a calmer interface, a more focused alternative is worth considering.
