If a PDF has come from a scanner, it's almost guaranteed to have at least one of these problems: a sideways page, a blank page, or pages in the wrong order.

## 1. Get a page-by-page view first

Open the PDF in a tool that shows page thumbnails so you can spot rotated pages, blanks, duplicates, and pages out of order.

## 2. Rotate pages

1. Open ClarixPDF's organize tool at clarixpdf.com/organize.
2. Upload the PDF — you'll see thumbnails of every page.
3. Click the rotate icon on any sideways page.
4. Repeat for each affected page.

## 3. Reorder and delete pages

- **Reorder:** drag thumbnails into the correct sequence.
- **Delete:** select blank or duplicate pages and remove them.

## 4. Save and verify

After making changes, scroll through the entire result and confirm the page count matches your expectations.

## When to extract pages instead

If you only need a few pages from a large document, use the extract option rather than deleting everything else — it's faster.
